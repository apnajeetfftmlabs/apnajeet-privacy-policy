ApnaJeet Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

ApnaJeet, developed by FFTM Labs, respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Google account name, email, and user ID (for authentication)
  • Game UID and in-app profile data (for tournament participation)
  • Device info for account binding & security

How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users via Google Firebase
  • Store tournament participation and leaderboard records
  • Process manual reward and withdrawal requests

Data Security

All data is securely stored in Google Firebase with AES-256 encryption. No payment or sensitive personal data is stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage)
  • Google AdMob (Rewarded Ads)

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Deletion or Support

To delete your data or request removal, email us at: apnajeet.fftmlabs@gmail.com

Legal Compliance

ApnaJeet is a skill-based eSports tournament platform. It does not promote gambling, betting, or chance-based gaming of any kind.
